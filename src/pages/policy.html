PRIVACY POLICY TERM OF USE

1. DATA ACQUISITION & TELEMETRY OVERVIEW

Welcome to OMNI GEAR. In our pursuit of delivering elite gaming hardware and an uncompromised "Omni Universe" experience, we collect and process specific data packets. By initializing your uplink with our platform, you consent to the data collection protocols outlined in this document. We treat your privacy with the same tactical precision as our hardware engineering.

2. SPECIFIC DATA CATEGORIES

To optimize your gaming ecosystem, we acquire the following information:

  • Identity Packets: Your legal name, encrypted account credentials, and verified email address used for system authentication.
  • Logistical Telemetry: Physical shipping coordinates, billing addresses, and contact digits required for gear deployment and hardware delivery.
  • Hardware & System Data: IP addresses, MAC addresses, browser architecture, and device specifications (CPU/GPU/RAM) to ensure our web interface is perfectly calibrated for your machine.
  • Interaction Data: Click-maps, session duration, and product interests used to refine the Omni Universe interface.

3. SECURE PROCESSING & FINANCIAL PROTECTION

Security is a core directive of the Omni Command Center. All data transmissions are shielded by 256-bit SSL encryption.

  • Financial Integrity: We utilize verified, third-party payment gateways. OMNI GEAR INC. does not store raw credit card digits or CVV codes on local servers.
  • Encryption: Your personal data is stored in partitioned, encrypted cloud nodes to prevent unauthorized access.

4. DATA RETENTION & DELETION PROTOCOLS

We retain your information only as long as your Operative Profile remains active. Should you choose to terminate your uplink:

  • Inactive Profiles: Accounts inactive for more than 24 solar months are automatically purged from our primary database.
  • Permanent Erasure: You may trigger a "Total Wipe" of your personal data at any time by contacting our support nexus.

5. THIRD-PARTY UPLINKS & LOGISTICS

Omni Gear does not sell your data to third-party entities. We only share essential data packets with trusted partners necessary for operation:

  • Deployment Partners: Global couriers (DHL, FedEx, etc.) receive your logistical data for gear delivery.
  • Analytic Nodes: We use anonymized data to analyze global gaming trends and improve Omni Gear product lines.

6. COOKIE DEPLOYMENT & TRACKING

Our system deploys "Cookies"—small data beacons—to remember your session parameters, cart inventory, and preferred RGB UI settings. You may disable these beacons via your browser’s security settings, but be advised that doing so may result in "System Errors" and limited functionality within the Omni Gear shop.

7. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

As a global entity, OMNI GEAR INC. may transfer data across international borders to our central processing hubs. By using our services, you acknowledge that your data may be stored in jurisdictions with different privacy laws than your home territory.

8. MINOR PROTECTION PROTOCOL

The Omni Universe is designed for operatives aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect telemetry from individuals under the age of 13. If our system detects an unauthorized minor uplink, the associated data will be immediately incinerated.

9. AMENDMENTS TO THE PRIVACY SHIELD

Omni Gear reserves the right to "Hotpatch" this Privacy Policy as global regulations evolve. Any critical updates will be broadcasted to your primary communication node (email). Continued use of Omni Gear services post-update constitutes acceptance of the new privacy parameters.
